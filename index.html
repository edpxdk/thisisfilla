About the Band

Our Story

Filla is an alternative rock project from Bali, Indonesia, founded by Surya Aufilla Ramadhan (Kasur). What began as a personal vision later evolved into a unique musical journey involving talented visually impaired musicians who shared the same passion for creating powerful and meaningful music. Inspired by bands such as Arctic Monkeys, Måneskin, and Avenged Sevenfold, Filla blends energetic rock elements with honest storytelling and emotional depth. Through every song, Filla aims to prove that limitations should never define a person's ability to create, dream, and inspire.

More than just a music project, Filla represents resilience, determination, and the belief that every voice deserves to be heard. From stories of heartbreak and personal growth to themes of strength and perseverance, Filla continues to turn real-life experiences into music that connects with listeners beyond boundaries.
